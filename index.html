How to make a clipping mask on Adobe Illustrator

Assembly time: 5 minutes
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

Are you wondering how to make a cool clipping mask that can easily elevate an Adobe Illustrator design? It's really interesting to make. Why don't you try to make it yourself? Follow the steps and you'll make a great clipping mask!

  • Things You'll Need

  • PC or Mac Computer
  • Adobe Illustrator Software
  • A computer mouse

  1. Open Adobe Illustrator and hit new file for a new image or open file for an existing one.

    Long-click and release on the "shape" tool. It's just below the text tool (T) near the top of the toolbar on the left side of the screen.

  2. Click on a tool. Choose a tool to draw the shape of the mask you want to use on the image.

    Draw the object you'd like to use as a mask. Do so by clicking on and holding somewhere on the screen, and then dragging the tool's crosshairs to create the size and shape you desire.

    "

    Collage art that was made with clipping masks on Adobe Illustrator.

  3. Click on the black pointer tool. It's in the upper-left corner of the tool bar.

    Place the vector shape over the image. Do so by clicking on and dragging the vector shape until the portion of the image you want to remain visible is inside the shape you drew.

  4. Hit CTRL + A or Command + A. Doing so selects all the objects in the window.

    Right click. A pop up menu will appear.

  5. Click on make clipping mask. Your image will be clipped to the shape of the object you created.

    And now you've created a clipping mask! Happy designing!
